About UpStream Global Services

UpStream Global Services is a leading engineering, technology and business consulting company that specializes in services that help our clients achieve their development, improvement and growth objectives.

We are a division of an international technology consulting firm with over 22 years experience. Our broad geographic presence ensures that proven and reliable expert resources are available and deployable throughout North American and in select regions worldwide.


Quality CMM Inspection, Programming - OGP

We have an immediate opportunity for a Quality CMM Inspection, Programming - OGP  to support one of our F500 clients in the Franklin Lakes, NJ area.

The successful candidate will be responsible for implementing inspection related activities to support the R&D group in the New Product Development and Introduction process, 
Responsibilities include, but not limited to the following: 
• Prepares, debugs and optimizes inspection programs on an OGP and scanning CMM machine using Measuremind, MeasureX and Calypso software; 
• Collaborates with multiple R&D teams on metrology and inspection methods and conducts technical and feasibility studies; 
• Analyze new product prints during their development to identify the suitable inspection technologies at the early stages of the projects; 
• Lends technical expertise to help plan and conduct engineering and metrology research including the identification, investigation and implementation of new advanced metrology and inspection systems and technologies; 
• Will determine and implement system design and programming approaches and parameters and will participate in Gage R&R studies. 
Candidate must be proficient in Measuremind, MeasureX and/or Calypso software packaging for programming palletized measurements.
